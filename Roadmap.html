Internship Application Process

Internship Application Process

  1. Apply for the Internship

    Begin by submitting your application for the internship program. You can do this through our online application form.

  2. Our Team Reviews the Application

    Our team will review your application to ensure that you meet the eligibility criteria and that your application is complete.

  3. A Test Link is Provided for the Selection Process

    If your application is successful, we will provide you with a test link to evaluate your skills and knowledge in the relevant domain.

  4. Team Meeting

    Our team may conduct a meeting to discuss the applications and test results, and to select the candidates who will proceed to the next step.

  5. Offer Letter Provided from Our Company

    If you are selected, you will receive an offer letter from our company, officially inviting you to join our internship program.

  6. Project Allocation

    After accepting the offer, you will be assigned to a project or team where you will gain hands-on experience in your chosen domain.

  7. Virtual Meeting Every Week

    Participate in weekly virtual meetings to discuss your progress, receive guidance, and collaborate with other interns and team members.

  8. Task Submission

    Submit tasks and projects as assigned during the internship period, showcasing your skills and learning in a real-world setting.

  9. Verification of Task Submission

    Our team will review and verify your task submissions, providing feedback and guidance to help you improve your skills.

  10. Receipt of Internship Certificate and Letter of Recommendation (LOR)

    Upon successful completion of the internship, you will receive an internship certificate and a letter of recommendation to boost your career prospects.

  11. Swags Winner Declaration

    We will declare winners of our swag contests, where you have a chance to win exciting prizes based on your performance during the internship.
