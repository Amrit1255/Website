Client Engagement Process at Kritark Solutions

Client Engagement Process at Kritark Solutions

  1. Initial Consultation

    The client reaches out to us through our contact channels. We schedule an initial consultation to understand their requirements, business goals, and challenges.

  2. Requirement Analysis and Proposal

    Our team reviews the client's needs, conducts a thorough analysis, and prepares a customized proposal detailing the scope of work, timeline, and budget.

  3. Client Agreement and Contract Signing

    Once the client approves the proposal, we finalize the agreement and sign the contract, ensuring all terms are clear and agreed upon.

  4. Project Kickoff

    After the agreement, we initiate the project by assigning a dedicated team of experts who will collaborate closely with the client throughout the process.

  5. Planning and Strategy Development

    Our team develops a detailed project plan, defines key deliverables, and sets up milestones for regular progress checks.

  6. Execution of IT Services

    We begin implementing the agreed-upon IT services, whether it's software development, system integration, cloud solutions, or consulting, with continuous monitoring and feedback loops.

  7. Regular Progress Updates

    Weekly or bi-weekly meetings are scheduled to update the client on progress, address any concerns, and align on the next steps.

  8. Quality Assurance and Testing

    We conduct thorough quality assurance and testing phases to ensure that all deliverables meet the clientâ€™s requirements and industry standards.

  9. Final Delivery and Handover

    Upon successful completion of the project, we deliver the final product or service to the client, ensuring all aspects are functional and ready for deployment.

  10. Client Review and Feedback

    We encourage clients to review the delivered solutions, provide feedback, and suggest any final adjustments or improvements.

  11. Ongoing Support and Maintenance

    After the project is completed, we offer ongoing support and maintenance services to ensure smooth operation and continuous improvement.
